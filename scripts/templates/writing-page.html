About the Writing

Logic. Narrative. Possession.

I build with logic, but I communicate through narrative. If you’ve seen the code side of my work, you know I value precision. Here, I value transparency.

I am informal by nature—a talkative person who often shares personal details others might keep hidden. My work is shaped by deep, persistent obsessions: a twenty-year love for Lea, a fascination with post-modern aesthetics, and my perspective as April.

The IAT Manual Project

The document currently on this page is a placeholder. I am reimagining the IAT Software manual by merging the tactile with the digital. Using professionally bound copies and two-up photostats, I am building a 3D-interactive experience that captures the weight of the physical object.

Whether I'm documenting complex systems or exploring the personas of my creative work, I am looking for the point where technical mastery meets raw human emotion.
